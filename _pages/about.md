My interest is to study GPU-friendly sampling, diffusion, filtering, and transport methods. The applications include, but not limited to generative models and time series. I actively contribute to open-source projects such as [Contour Sampler in JAX](https://github.com/blackjax-devs/blackjax) and [NLP in Finance](https://github.com/WayneDW/Sentiment-Analysis-in-Event-Driven-Stock-Price-Movement-Prediction).
